Here are the available cake design categories depending on the advance notice:

  • Ready in 48+ hours: Dessert Cakes, Quick Cakes, Simply "Bake N cakes" Cakes, Two-Tier Cakes & all of our all-occasion cake designs shown on our website
  • Ready in 7 days: All cakes available including custom tiered, 3D, cut-outs, shaped, etc. (but limited by baking capacity and capabilities so please order as early as possible

If you would like a quote for cake that is not shown on our website, please send an email containing the following information (please feel free to copy this list into the email and write your answers next to each question):
  • What is your name and phone number?
  • What is the date and time you would need to pick-up the cake?
  • What cake flavor(s) would you like? (Note if there are multiple tiers please include the flavors for each tier)
  • What filling(s) would you like? (Note if there are multiple tiers please include the fillings for each tier)
  • What frosting would you like?
  • Attach the example picture of the cake you would like quoted
  • Note any changes you would like made to the design of the cake using the attached picture as a the reference.
  • If the cake has fondant (smooth covering or 3D pieces on the cake), let us know if you want the fondant on your cake or prefer buttercream.
  • How many people do you need to serve?
  • If the cake would end up being smaller than what you have pictured based on your servings is that okay OR do you want the quote to be as close as possible to the picture regardless of your required serving sizes?
We require your cake order be paid in full at the time the cake order is placed. Please note your cake order will not be fulfilled if you do not pay for the cake in advance. We do accept credit cards over the phone and are able to email you the receipt for your convenience.
Cake orders can be cancelled with at least an 8 day notice and the customer will be issued a full refund. However there are no cancellations or refunds allowed within 7 days of the original pick-up date of the cake order.
We do make a cakes that can include your personal picture as part of the design and a few of these options can be found on our “Edible Image Cakes” page. We require that you either upload the picture when you order your cake or email us the image you would like to us at info@aggiesbakery.com. As part of our quality control process, we do not accept photos or pictures in the store. A simple alternative to scanning your image would be take a picture of the image in high resolution on your cell phone and email us the photo directly to us. Lastly, we will not make accept any pictures that include registered TRADEMARKS. Any image that includes a trademark would require authorization from the owner of the Trademark in order for us to use the image as part of the cake decoration. Please see our FAQ regarding Trademark specific cakes for more details.
